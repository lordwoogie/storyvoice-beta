Save this voice to your device

This voice is precious. We strongly recommend saving a backup copy to your own device. If this voice belongs to someone who has passed or is elderly, this backup ensures their voice is never lost.

How to keep it safe:

On your phone: The file saves to your Downloads folder. Move it to a cloud service like iCloud, Google Drive, or Dropbox for extra safety.
On your computer: Save it to your Documents folder AND copy it to a USB flash drive or external hard drive.
Best practice: Keep copies in at least 2 places. Email the file to yourself as an extra backup. Label it clearly (e.g. "Grandma Betty voice recording 2026").
Share with family: Send the file to other family members so multiple people have a copy. This voice belongs to your whole family.
